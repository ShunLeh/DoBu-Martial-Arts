Facilities at Dobu Martial Arts

At Dobu Martial Arts, we are committed to providing a welcoming and well-equipped environment that fosters optimal learning, training, and growth for our students. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to cater to the diverse needs of martial artists of all ages and skill levels. When you step into our dojo, you'll find an array of facilities to support your journey in martial arts.

Parking

We offer ample parking space to accommodate our students and visitors, ensuring convenience and ease of access to our facility.

Changing Rooms and Locker Facilitie

Our dojo is equipped with clean and well-maintained changing rooms to ensure your comfort before and after training sessions. Locker facilities are available for you to store your belongings securely during class, allowing you to focus on your training without worry.

Meditation and Relaxation Space

Martial arts training is not just about physical techniques; it also involves mental and emotional development. To facilitate mindfulness and relaxation, we have a dedicated space for meditation and quiet reflection, where students can recharge and find balance in their busy lives.

Martial Arts Library

Our dojo's martial arts library houses a collection of books, videos, and resources related to various martial arts styles, philosophy, and history. Students are welcome to explore these resources to deepen their understanding and appreciation of martial arts.

Wi-Fi Accessg

We provide complimentary Wi-Fi access within our facility, allowing students and visitors to stay connected and access online martial arts resources when needed.

Martial Arts Equipment

We provide a wide range of martial arts equipment to enhance your training. From focus pads, kick shields, and heavy bags for striking practice to training weapons and grappling dummies for specialized training, our facility has the tools you need to improve your techniques and build your confidence.

Pro Shop

Our pro shop offers a selection of martial arts gear, equipment, and Dobu Martial Arts merchandise. From uniforms and belts to protective gear and training accessories, you can find everything you need to support your training journey.
