About us

Welcome to Dobu Martial Arts, a place where ancient traditions meet modern training methods to empower individuals physically, mentally, and spiritually. Our dojo is more than just a martial arts school; it is a community dedicated to fostering personal growth, discipline, and respect for oneself and others.

Welcome Domu Martial Atrs

Dobu Martial Arts was started by a really good martial artist named Master Ryuji Nakamura. He wanted to create a place where people of all ages and backgrounds could learn martial arts in a happy and peaceful environment. At Dobu Martial Arts, we believe in important values like respect, humility, perseverance, and honesty. We think that learning martial arts is not just about doing cool moves, but also about discovering more about yourself and always getting better. We want our students to learn discipline, focus, and determination, not just in class, but also in their everyday lives. In our karate program, students learn how to hit, block, and kick properly. They also get stronger, faster, and more coordinated while learning about traditional karate.
