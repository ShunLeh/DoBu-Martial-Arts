Dobu Martial Ats Privacy Policy

At Dobu Martial Arts, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide us when interacting with our website, services, and programs. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection

We may collect personal information from you when you voluntarily provide it while registering for classes, workshops, or events. This information may include but is not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and age. We may also collect non-personal information, such as your IP address and browser type, for statistical purposes and to enhance your browsing experience on our website.

Use of Information

We use the information you provide to deliver our services effectively, respond to inquiries, and improve our offerings. Your personal information will only be used for the specific purposes for which you provided it, such as class scheduling, event registration, and communication with instructors and fellow students. We may also use your contact details to send you newsletters, updates, and promotional materials related to Dobu Martial Arts, but you have the option to opt-out of receiving such communications.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use industry-standard encryption and secure socket layer technology to safeguard sensitive data during transmission. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in delivering our services (e.g., payment processing, email delivery). These third-party partners are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, regulation, or in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or government request.

Children's Privacyn

Protecting the privacy of children is essential to us. Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information from our records.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and improve our services. You can modify your browser settings to disable cookies; however, this may affect the functionality and usability of our website.
